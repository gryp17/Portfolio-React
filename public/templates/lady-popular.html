Browser-based, free-to-play, online fashion game with more than 8 million registered players.

I have been involved with the maintenance and development of both the backend and frontend functionalities of the game.

The game consists in collecting clothes and accesories for your wardrobe and your apartment in order to gain more popularity, ingame currency and experience. There are a lot of features such as:
  • Guilds
  • Night clubs
  • Duels with other ladies
  • Parties
  • Pets
  • Beauty contests
  • Events containing mini games
