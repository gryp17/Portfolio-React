M&S Géstion is a client and appointment management software created for the Spanish hair salon company "M&S Peluquerías". It consists of 3 separate modules - one for clients management, one for appointments and the data export module.

Here are some of their features:
  • Central database for all modules
  • Different user types with different privileges
  • Client/Appointment creation/modification/deletion
  • History of all appointmets
  • History of all services or products bought with their respective bonus points
  • Bonus and discount system
  • Export to CSV for all registered clients
