Sentiment Metrics is a social customer service platform. I have been responsible for the development of the new NodeJS/AngularJS version of the product. The web application allows you to control all your social networks providing the following features:
  • unified inbox for all social accounts
  • statistics
  • scheduled posts
  • configurable automatic responses and filtering

It supports all popular social networks like:
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+
  • Youtube
  • Linkedin
  • Instagram
  • Odnoklassniki
  • VKontakte
  • WeChat
